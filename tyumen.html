Established in 1963, Tyumen State Medical University has grown into one of Russia's leading medical institutions. Located in the heart of Siberia, it is a hub for medical innovation, research, and high-quality clinical training.


As a public university, we offer a specialized 6-year General Medicine (MBBS) program in English, designed specifically for international students to succeed globally.
