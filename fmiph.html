Legacy of
Medical Excellence

Founded in 1991 in the historic city of Fergana, FMIPH stands as a beacon of medical education in Central Asia. As a government state university, we combine rich academic tradition with modern medical infrastructure.


We are committed to fostering the next generation of global healthcare leaders through our comprehensive English-medium General Medicine program, directly affiliated with competitive international standards.
